FLIP PRIVACY POLICY

We're committed to protecting your privacy while providing the best productivity experience.

How Flip Uses Your Data

Flip uses motion tracking and location data to enhance your productivity experience. We do not sell your data to third parties or share it with unauthorized entities.

Flip uses Firebase for authentication and analytics. Firebase does not sell your data, only processes it to improve app performance.

Users can request their data be deleted by emailing jex@jajajeev.com. Requests will be processed within 10 days.

Motion Tracking (Required)

Motion tracking is a core part of Flip's functionality and is required for the app to work properly:

  • Tracks motion periodically to ensure your phone remains flipped over during productivity sessions.
  • Optimized for battery efficiency (runs only when needed).
  • Only active during productivity sessions and once upon installation (to request motion permissions).
  • Motion data is not stored or transmitted beyond what's needed for the app to function.

Location Tracking (Optional)

Location tracking is not required to use Flip, but enabling it enhances core features and unlocks additional functionality:

✅
Allows sessions to run while your screen is off (background processing)
✅
Regional & Building-Specific Leaderboards – Compete with others in the same location (e.g., libraries, cafés)
✅
FlipMaps – See friends' live Flip sessions and their last 3 historical sessions

Note: Without location tracking, Flip works normally, but you won't have access to leaderboards or FlipMaps.

FlipMaps & Data Storage

FlipMaps only displays your last 3 sessions and your current live session when active:

  • Data is automatically removed after 7 days (resets every Monday at 3 AM).
  • Your current location is NOT shared unless you are in an active Flip session.
  • Your sessions are only placed on the map once you initially open FlipMaps and read the privacy statement popup.

Visibility Controls

🔹 No One – Completely disables historical session visibility
🔹 Friends Only (default) – Only your friends can see your last 3 session locations
🔹 Everyone – Your last 3 session locations are visible to all users

Leaderboards & Privacy Controls

Types of Leaderboards

  • Building-Specific Leaderboards – Tracks weekly session counts for individual buildings
  • Regional Leaderboards – Compete based on total weekly session time in your area
  • Global Leaderboards – Compete with users worldwide

Building-Specific Leaderboards

This is a leaderboard of weekly number of sessions for every specific building in the world. It does not automatically assign a building to a user upon installation—a building has to be picked manually upon first usage.

Every session's coordinates use geocoding to assign itself a Building ID, prioritizing buildings where users have previously held sessions, and buildings with common keywords (e.g., "Library", "coffee", "study", "office").

Privacy Controls

You have complete control over your leaderboard privacy:

  • Anonymous Mode – Appear on leaderboards without showing your username or profile picture
  • Opt-Out – Completely remove yourself from all leaderboards

Data Resets

  • Weekly Leaderboards reset every Monday at 3 AM
  • Building session counts are cleared weekly
  • All-Time Regional Leaderboards do not store individual session locations—rankings are based on weekly session time within a given region

Your Privacy, Your Choice

Flip is designed with privacy-first principles. You are in complete control of your data:

  • Disable location tracking at any time
  • Choose who can see your session locations
  • Anonymize your profile on leaderboards
  • Opt out of leaderboards entirely
  • Turn off friend failure notifications

All privacy controls are available in the main Settings menu and in the Regional settings screen.
